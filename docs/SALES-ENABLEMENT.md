# Sales Enablement Pack (v1)

## 30-second pitch
"Most small businesses lose leads in the first 5 minutes. CRMs store leads, but they don’t guarantee fast response. We fix that. Our platform captures inbound leads, auto-responds immediately, keeps a simple follow-up inbox, and gives daily conversion visibility. It works alongside your existing CRM, LOS, or POS—so you improve results without replacing your stack."

## Discovery questions
1. How quickly are new leads contacted today?
2. How many leads are missed after hours or weekends?
3. Where do inbound leads come from right now?
4. What percent of leads become booked calls?
5. Which system must remain your source of truth (CRM/LOS/POS)?

## Objection handling
- **"We already have HubSpot/Salesforce."**
  - Great. We’re not replacing it—we make first-response speed and follow-up performance better, then sync updates back.

- **"My team won’t learn another complicated tool."**
  - This is intentionally simple: New/Contacted/Booked/Closed plus automation toggle.

- **"We can just do this manually."**
  - Manual works until volume spikes or after-hours leads arrive. We prevent lead leakage automatically.

## Pilot offer ideas
### Offer A — 30-day Speed-to-Lead Pilot
- Setup + templates + daily digest
- Weekly optimization check-ins
- Success target: median first response under 5 minutes

### Offer B — Rescue Missed Leads Pilot
- Focus on after-hours + uncontacted leads
- Escalation alerts + daily exceptions report
- Success target: reduce untouched leads by 70%+

### Offer C — Conversion Lift Pilot
- Response templates tuned per niche
- Follow-up cadence + booking link optimization
- Success target: measurable booked-call lift in 30 days

## KPI scorecard for pilots
- Median first response time
- % leads contacted within 5 minutes
- Lead-to-booked-call conversion rate
- # leads untouched >24h
