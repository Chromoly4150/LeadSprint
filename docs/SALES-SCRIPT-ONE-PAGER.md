# Sales Script (One Pager)

## Who this is for
Small businesses getting inbound leads from forms, calls, ads, or referrals and losing deals due to slow follow-up.

## 30-second opener
"Most small businesses don’t lose leads because of bad service—they lose them in the first 5 minutes. CRMs store leads, but they don’t guarantee fast response. We fix that. Our platform captures inbound leads, auto-responds right away, keeps follow-up simple, and helps you book more calls. It works with your existing CRM/LOS/POS, so there’s no rip-and-replace."

---

## Discovery flow (ask in this order)
1. "Where do your inbound leads come from today?"
2. "How quickly is a new lead usually contacted?"
3. "Do leads ever sit overnight or through weekends?"
4. "Roughly what percent become booked calls?"
5. "Which system has to stay your source of truth (CRM/LOS/POS)?"

## Pain amplification (if needed)
- "If response time drifts from 5 minutes to 30+, conversion usually drops hard."
- "Most teams think they respond quickly until they actually measure it."

---

## Core pitch
"We give you a speed-to-lead operating layer:
- instant lead capture,
- immediate first response,
- simple pipeline (New / Contacted / Booked / Closed),
- daily visibility into missed or slow follow-ups.

So your team can focus on closing, not chasing lead chaos."

---

## Objection handling (verbatim)

### "We already use HubSpot/Salesforce."
"Perfect. We’re not replacing it. We sit on top and improve first-response speed and follow-up execution, then sync key updates back."

### "My team won’t learn another complicated tool."
"That’s exactly what we avoid. This is intentionally lightweight and focused on the first 5 minutes and booked outcomes."

### "We can just do this manually."
"Manual works until volume spikes or after-hours leads come in. We prevent lead leakage automatically and show you exactly where follow-up is slipping."

---

## Pilot close
"Let’s run a 30-day pilot. We’ll set up your lead sources, templates, and daily reporting. If we can’t materially improve response speed and show booked-call lift, you walk."

## Pilot success criteria
- Median first response under 5 minutes
- Higher % of leads contacted within 5 minutes
- Measurable lead-to-booked-call lift
- Fewer untouched leads after 24 hours

---

- "Want to see a 10-minute walkthrough with your actual lead flow?"
- "If I map this to your current stack this week, can we start pilot setup Monday?"
- "Who on your team owns inbound lead response day-to-day so we can include them in setup?"
