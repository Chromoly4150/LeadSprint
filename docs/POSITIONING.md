# Positioning: LeadSprint

## Category
A **Speed-to-Lead Conversion Layer** for small businesses.

## What we are not
- Not a full CRM replacement
- Not an enterprise workflow suite
- Not a generic contact database

## Core Promise
**Respond to every inbound lead in under 5 minutes and convert more leads into booked calls.**

## Primary Differentiator
Most CRMs are systems of record. We are a **system of response speed + conversion execution**.

## Messaging Pillars
1. **Fast first response, every time**
2. **Simple inbox for non-technical business owners**
3. **Automation focused on booked outcomes, not feature sprawl**
4. **Works with existing tools (CRM/LOS/POS), no rip-and-replace**

## One-liner options
1. "The fastest way to respond to new leads and book more calls."
2. "Your 5-minute lead response engine—built for small businesses."
3. "We sit on top of your existing tools and turn inbound leads into booked appointments faster."

**Headline:** Convert more inbound leads by responding in under 5 minutes.
